12 Types of Tenses in Hindi & English
Tense Sentence (Hindi) Sentence (English Translation)
Present Simple मैं रोज स्कूल जाता हूँ। I go to school daily.
Present Continuous मैं अभी स्कूल जा रहा हूँ। I am going to school now.
Present Perfect मैं स्कूल जा चुका हूँ। I have gone to school.
Present Perfect Continuous मैं सुबह से स्कूल जा रहा हूँ। I have been going to school since morning.
Past Simple मैं कल स्कूल गया था। I went to school yesterday.
Past Continuous मैं कल दोपहर को स्कूल जा रहा था। I was going to school in the afternoon yesterday.
Past Perfect मैं स्कूल जा चुका था जब क्लास शुरू हुई। I had gone to school when the class started.
Past Perfect Continuous मैं दो घंटे से स्कूल जा रहा था जब बारिश हुई। I had been going to school for two hours when it rained.
Future Simple मैं कल स्कूल जाऊँगा। I will go to school tomorrow.
Future Continuous मैं कल इस वक्त स्कूल जा रहा होऊँगा। I will be going to school at this time tomorrow.
Future Perfect मैं कल स्कूल जा चुका होऊँगा जब तुम आओगे। I will have gone to school by the time you arrive.
Future Perfect Continuous मैं तीन घंटे से स्कूल जा रहा होऊँगा जब तुम आओगे। I will have been going to school for three hours when you arrive.
